Pokemon Collector!

You will be given a random number at the start of the game.


There are four Pokemons below. By clicking on a Pokemon you will add a specifc amount of Damage points to your total damage score.


You win the game by matching your total damage score to the random number, you lose the game if your total damage score goes above the random number.


The damage value of each Pokemon is hidden from you until you click on it.


Each time when the game starts, the game will change the damage values of each Pokemon.
